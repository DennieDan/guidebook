# 💌 Getting-to-Know-You Form

This is one of the very important first steps to prepare for your mentoring journey with your mentees. At the very first meet-up between your mentees and you, you can ask your supervisor/mentees' teacher to collect some necessary information from them by sending them a digital form. We call it a **Getting-to-Know-You Form**. It is the chance for you to know their likes, hates, preferences, hobbies, strengths and weaknesses so as to make a better proposal.

Make sure you do not try to collect their personal information. Here are some suggested questions for your **Getting-to-Know-You Form**

<box type="tip" header="##### Tips">
<markdown>

- Prefer checkbox, multiple-choice questions to short response and long response questions.
- Give them choices for selection and add an <i>Others</i> option. Try to think of as many relevant options as possible to give them more context to think about what they really like.
- Making mentees writing answer usually ends up with a lot of _blanks_ or _NA_ responses.
  </markdown>
  </box>
