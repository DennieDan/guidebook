Role Responsibilities

Lead Mentor

  • Primary facilitator for the session
  • Responsible for content delivery and main activities
  • Manages the overall flow and timing

Support Mentor

  • Assists the lead mentor during activities
  • Provides additional guidance to mentees
  • Steps in if lead mentor is unavailable

Logistic Manager

  • Handles venue, equipment, and technical setup
  • Manages refreshments and materials
  • Coordinates with venue staff if applicable

Mentee Coordinator

  • Manages mentee attendance and engagement
  • Handles mentee-specific concerns or questions
  • Coordinates communication with mentees

Backup Plan

  • Alternative approaches if primary plans fail
  • Contingency measures for various scenarios
  • Emergency contact information

Tips for Effective Timeline Management

  • Buffer time: Include 5-10 minute buffers between major activities
  • Flexibility: Be prepared to adjust timing based on mentee engagement
  • Communication: Ensure all team members have the timeline in advance
  • Documentation: Keep notes on what worked well for future sessions
